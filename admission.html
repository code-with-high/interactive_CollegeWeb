About Us

Welcome Acharya Motibhai Patel Institute of Computer Studies was established in 1999 by Gujarat State Education Trust. It is a pioneer, self-financing, co-educational Institution, situated in serene green surroundings at Kherva, Gujarat. It is affiliated to the Ganpat University.he qualified and experienced faculty at college along with the carefully designed and updated curriculum ensure that the students receive the best education and skills which can help them emerge as qualified professionals
