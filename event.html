About Us

Acharya Motibhai Patel Institute of Computer Studies is situated in Mehsana in Gujarat state of India. Established in 1999, AMPICS is a Private college. The college is accredited by AICTE. Acharya Motibhai Patel Institute of Computer Studies offers 2 courses across 1 streams namely IT..Popular degrees offered at Acharya Motibhai Patel Institute of Computer Studies include BCA+MCA, MCA..Besides a robust teaching pedagogy, Acharya Motibhai Patel Institute of Computer Studies is also a leader in research and innovation. . The placement at Acharya Motibhai Patel Institute of Computer Studies is varied, with recruitment options both in corporates and public sector as well as entrepreneurship.
