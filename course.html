Course

B.E(Bachelor of Engineering)

The core curriculum in education at MCA levels and also have btech and much more. A candidate seeking admission on All India Seats shall apply online, on the University website(admission.guni.ac.in), within the prescribed time limit. Twenty-five percent of seats shall be filled by the University from the candidates who have passed the Qualifying Examination from the schools located in India (including Gujarat State) and have appeared in JEE (Main) 2022. The merit list for these seats shall be based on marks obtained in JEE (Main) 2022.

Eligibility for Admission

A citizen of India who has passed any Bachelor Degree examination of Bangalore University or any University in India or aboard recognized as equivalent through 10+2+3 pattern of education obtaining 50% of marks in aggregate of all the subjects.
